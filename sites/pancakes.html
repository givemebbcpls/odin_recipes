Pancakes

Pancake is not so easy like other meals, but not hard. All of us love tthis tasty circles with some syrup.

Ingridients:

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tblspn oil

Steps:

  1. Put flour, eggs, milk, oil and pinch of salt into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Heat a pan over medium fire.
  3. Cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden.
