Sandwich

Sandwich is the most easy to cook meal. The best for breakfast, and for so positive quality everybody have to know how to cook it.

Ingridients:

  • Two bread's slices
  • Some vegetables, like tomato, salad, cucumber
  • Ham or somthing
  • Some sause (e.g. mayonese, ketchup)

Steps:

  1. Sauce bread's slices
  2. Chop and add vegetables
  3. Add ham
  4. Put on a grill
